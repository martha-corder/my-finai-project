Background

Biyinzika was founded in 1990 as a poultry breeder farm and has grown steadily over the years to establish itself as one of the best- known brands in the Ugandan poultry industry.
We are now the leading supplier of day-old chicks and poultry feeds in the country.
We have recently started producing other livestock feeds such as dairy and pig feeds.
A commercial broiler farm was set up in 2016 which is now in full production.

What We Do

Breeder Farms and Hatchery

Located around Mukono can produce upto 300,000 broiler eggs per week, 100,000 layer eggs per week.

Retail network

Our chicks and feed are sold throughout the country through our network of 34 branches.
Our customers range from large scale commercial farmers to small-scale farmers who rear as little as 100 birds.
We have adopted a uniform pricing policy across the country in order to encourage development of the industry.
A team of veterinary qualified staff provide technical support to all our customers.
